# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Clients
Client profiles with contracts, contacts, and onboarding status for all active engagements.

### 2. Projects
Project tracking with phases, deliverables, milestones, and visual timeline per client.

### 3. Tasks
Task and stakeholder management with risks, questions, and engagement tracking.

### 4. Dashboards
Auto-generated internal dashboards and client-facing status pages from live project data.

### 5. Invoicing
Integrated time tracking that flows directly into invoice generation.

### 6. Admin
User management, role-based access control, audit logging, and GDPR compliance.
